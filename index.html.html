I'm a full-stack developer and UI engineer with four years of experience designing and building products for startups and small teams. I care about the details that make an interface feel effortless: spacing, motion, contrast, and speed.

My work spans from marketing sites to full web applications. I enjoy owning a feature end-to-end — sketching the interface, building the front end, wiring up the back end, and shipping it to real users. When I'm not coding, I'm usually reading about design systems or tinkering with a side project.
